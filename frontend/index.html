How It Works

Understanding the AI diagnosis process

1. Submit Your Case

Describe your symptoms and optionally upload medical images. Our AI will analyze the information.

2. AI Analysis

Advanced AI models process your symptoms and images to identify potential conditions and patterns.

3. Doctor Review

Qualified doctors review the AI analysis and provide personalized medical prescriptions and guidance.

4. Get Prescription

Use your Patient ID to view your prescription and medical recommendations when they're ready.

Important Notice

This AI system is designed to assist healthcare professionals and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Help & Instructions

How to submit your case:

  1. Describe your symptoms in detail in the text area
  2. Optionally upload a medical image for visual analysis
  3. Click "Submit Case" to send your information
  4. Save your Patient ID - you'll need it later
  5. Check back using "View Prescription" to see your results

Tips for better results:

  • Be specific about symptoms and their severity
  • Include relevant medical history if available
  • Use clear, high-quality images when uploading
  • Provide context about when symptoms started
  • Keep your Patient ID safe for future reference

What happens next:

  • AI analyzes your symptoms and images
  • Doctors review the AI analysis
  • You receive a prescription and medical guidance
  • Use your Patient ID to access your results
